# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) ❓

## Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Here are answers to the most common questions people have about G4F.

## Getting Started

### **Q: What is G4F?**  
**A:** G4F is a free tool that lets you use powerful AI assistants without paying anything. It's like having a super-smart friend who's always available to help!

### **Q: Is it really free?**  
**A:** Yes! 100% free. No hidden costs, no credit card needed, no "freemium" traps. Just free AI.

### **Q: Do I need to create an account?**  
**A:** No! You can start using G4F immediately without signing up or creating an account.

### **Q: How do I start?**  
**A:** Just go to [https://g4f.dev/chat](https://g4f.dev/chat) and start typing! That's it!

### **Q: Do I need to install anything?**  
**A:** No! You can use it right in your web browser. If you want, you can also install it on your computer, but it's not necessary.

## Using G4F

### **Q: What can I ask G4F?**  
**A:** Almost anything! Questions, homework help, writing assistance, creative ideas, coding help, image creation, and more.

### **Q: What can't I ask?**  
**A:** Don't ask for:
- Harmful or dangerous advice
- Illegal activities
- Personal information about real people
- Hate speech or harassment

### **Q: How fast are the answers?**  
**A:** Usually 5-15 seconds for text, 10-30 seconds for images. Sometimes faster, sometimes a bit slower.

### **Q: Is there a limit to how much I can use?**  
**A:** No limits! Use it as much as you want, whenever you want.

### **Q: Can I have long conversations?**  
**A:** Yes! You can chat back and forth as much as you like. The AI will remember the conversation (within the same chat session).

## Privacy & Safety

### **Q: Is my conversation private?**  
**A:** Yes! Your conversations are not stored anywhere and are not shared with anyone.

### **Q: Do you save my data?**  
**A:** No. We don't store your conversations, messages, or any personal information.

### **Q: Is it safe for kids?**  
**A:** Yes! G4F is safe for children. The AI filters out inappropriate content. However, parental supervision is always recommended for young children.

### **Q: Can companies use G4F?**  
**A:** Yes! Many businesses use G4F for customer service, content creation, and more.

- Opera

### **Q: Why is it slow sometimes?**  
**A:** Sometimes the AI services are busy. Try again in a minute, or switch to a different AI model.

## Troubleshooting

### **Q: The page won't load. What do I do?**  
**A:** 
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Try a different browser
4. Wait a few minutes and try again

### **Q: The AI isn't responding. What's wrong?**  
**A:** 
1. Wait a bit longer (sometimes it's busy)
2. Try a different AI model
3. Refresh the page and try again
4. Check if your internet is working

### **Q: The answer doesn't make sense. Why?**  
**A:** 
1. Try rephrasing your question
2. Give more details
3. Ask for clarification: "Can you explain that more clearly?"
4. Try a different AI model

### **Q: Images aren't creating. Help!**  
**A:** 
1. Make sure you're using a model that creates images
2. Be more specific in your description
3. Try simpler images first
4. Try again - sometimes it fails temporarily

### **Q: I got an error message. What does it mean?**  
**A:** Error messages usually mean:
- The service is temporarily busy
- Your request was too complex
- There's a temporary technical issue

**Solution:** Wait a minute and try again, or simplify your request.
