**Yes, 100% free.**

No hidden costs. No "freemium" traps. No credit card required.

G4F doesn't run the AI itself. It's like a bridge that connects you to AI services that already exist. We're just making them easier to access.

Think of it like:
- A library gives you free access to books (they don't write the books)
